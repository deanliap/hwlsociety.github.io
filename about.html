About HWLS

Rooted in heritage.
Built for now.

The Hellenic Women's Leadership Society was founded in 2026 by women who saw a gap — a need for a dedicated professional home for Hellenic women in the Midwest.

We believe that community is strategy. That knowing the right women, at the right moment, in a space built for you — changes everything.

HWLS is a place to grow, to give back, and to be surrounded by women who understand both your ambition and your roots.

Our Core Values

Leadership Championing the advancement of Hellenic women in every sphere.
Integrity Honesty and accountability in all we do.
Community A welcoming space built on genuine connection.
Excellence High standards — for our work and for ourselves.
Heritage Honoring the culture and legacy that shapes us.
Respect Engaging every individual with dignity and openness.

Our Patron Saint

Under the guidance of
St. Xenia of St. Petersburg

HWLS has adopted St. Xenia of St. Petersburg as our patron saint — a woman of extraordinary courage who, in the face of profound loss, chose radical service over comfort and convention.

She surrendered her worldly desires to serve our God and her community. In doing so, she became one of the most beloved and powerful figures in the Orthodox tradition.

Her legacy reminds us that true leadership is built through unfading devotion to others.
